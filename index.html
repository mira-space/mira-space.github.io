MiraData

MiraData is a large-scale video dataset with long video duration and hierarchical caption. It is specifically designed for tasks such as long video generation and video completion. Each video in MiraData has an uncut duration ranging from 1 to 2 minutes, and hierarchical captions with an average length of 349 words describing the video from various aspects. Each video has 6 captions: main object description, background, style, camera move, short caption, and dense caption. Videos are crawled from the internet then cutted to clips and captioned with pretrained models.
MiraData is still in an early stage development and we only release two senarios in this version, gaming and city/scenic exploration.
